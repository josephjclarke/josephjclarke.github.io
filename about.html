About

I’m working towards a PhD in Climate Science at the University of Exeter, supervised by Peter Cox, Peter Ashwin and somewhat unofficially by Paul Ritchie.

My research falls into the field of climate dynamics. More specifically I’m interested in emergent constraints, tipping points and the links between the two. I use a hierarchy of models, from the qualitative (in a mathematical sense) insights of dynamical systems theory to complex global numerical models like JULES.
